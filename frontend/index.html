SoMA

by Group 7

Social Media Analyser - An awesome way to visualise social media data

This is the prototype of SoMA, Group 7's Social Media Data Visualisation Toolkit, a 2nd Year Group Project for Cardiff School of Computer Science and Informatics

It's a most excellent web-based method of displaying data gathered from social networks in new and interesting ways.

Using this toolkit, we hope to help uncover previously unseen trends, patterns, and views of existing data gathered by Cardiff Online Social Media Observatory (COSMOS for short)

(COSMOS is a joint project between Cardiff School of Computer Science and Informatics and Cardiff University School of Social Sciences)
